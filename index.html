Spiritual Knowledge Q&A (1–30)

Spiritual Knowledge & Life Lessons Q&A (1–30)

1. Who is God?
Life Story: A young shepherd named Daniel once felt alone after losing his father. One night while watching the stars, he whispered, “God, are You real?” A calm peace filled his heart. He later learned that God reveals Himself not through loud signs, but through His presence, guiding us quietly. Daniel’s faith grew, and he found strength to face life’s storms. This reflects how God makes Himself known to anyone who seeks Him sincerely.
Answer: God is the Creator of the universe, the Alpha and Omega, who made all things and sustains all life.
Bible Explanation: Genesis 1:1 teaches that in the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth — meaning everything finds its source in Him.
Life Lesson: Knowing who God is gives us identity and purpose. We are not accidents — we are created with intention.
2. Why did God create human beings?
Life Story: A mother once asked her child, “Why do you think I made you?” The child replied, “Because you wanted someone to love.” She smiled and said, “Exactly.” In the same way, God created us out of love, not necessity. We exist because love desired expression.
Answer: God created humans for relationship, fellowship, and to reflect His image.
Bible Explanation: Genesis 1:27 reminds us that we are made in God’s image — meaning we carry His nature of love, creativity, and purpose.
Life Lesson: You are valuable. Your life is not random. You were made intentionally and lovingly.

12. What does it mean to carry the cross as a disciple?

It means sacrificing personal desires and following God’s will.

Life Story

Ruth refused a corrupt job opportunity that paid well. She chose integrity, suffering financially for months. That was her cross—but God later blessed her honesty.

Bible Insight

Take up your cross daily and follow Christ. (Luke 9:23)

13. Who is a believer?

A believer has faith in Jesus and obeys His commandments.

Life Story

Angela attended church for years but lived carelessly. When she finally committed to obedience, her life changed—she became a true believer.

Bible Insight

Those who believe in Him become children of God. (John 1:12)

14. How is the devil a “believer”?

He believes God exists but rejects God’s authority.

Life Story

Peter once thought believing in God meant just acknowledging His existence. Later, he learned even demons believe—but true belief requires obedience.

Bible Insight

Even demons believe and tremble. (James 2:19)

15. What is the difference between true believers and the devil?

True believers obey God; the devil rebels.

Life Story

Beatrice struggled between worldly life and God’s word. When she chose obedience, she discovered true peace—something rebellion never gave her.

Bible Insight

Those who do righteousness are children of God. (1 John 3:10)

16. Why does the devil not love Jesus?

Because Jesus is perfectly obedient to God, something the devil hates.

Life Story

Paul noticed that whenever he prayed in Jesus’ name, temptations fled quickly. He realized darkness cannot love or tolerate light.

Bible Insight

Those who reject obedience cannot love Christ. (1 John 2:4)

17. What did Jesus say about love and obedience?

“If you love Me, keep My commandments.”

Life Story

Susan claimed she loved God but lived in sin. When she read John 14:15, she understood that love must be shown through obedience.

Bible Insight

Love and obedience are inseparable. (John 14:15)

18. How do we identify the devil’s followers?

By their rejection of God’s commandments and their deceitful works.

Life Story

Michael worked with people who mocked holiness and encouraged sin. He later learned such attitudes reflect the enemy’s influence.

Bible Insight

The devil disguises himself as an angel of light. (2 Corinthians 11:13–15)

19. What is the spiritual law of God?

God’s commandments guiding holiness and truth.

Life Story

When Janet started reading God’s commandments, she found answers to problems she didn’t understand. The spiritual law opened her eyes.

Bible Insight

The law is spiritual. (Romans 7:14)

20. Who discerns spiritual law?

Only those guided by the Holy Spirit.

Life Story

Before receiving the Holy Spirit, Ethan read the Bible like a storybook. Afterward, the Scriptures became alive and understandable.

Bible Insight

Spiritual things are spiritually discerned. (1 Corinthians 2:14)
